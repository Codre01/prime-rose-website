Admissions

The school is open to children between the ages of 6 months and 2 years for the pre-primary classes i.e. Reception 1, Reception 2, Nursery 1 &2.To be eligible for admission into Year 1, the child should be 5 years old by September of the same year.

TApplication forms and prospectus can be obtained from the school’s administrative office at a fee. Forms are to be completed and returned to the Head Teacher with all required documents, i.e;

Requirements

  • 1 recent passport photograph
  • 1 birth certificate (photocopy)
  • Last class report (where applicable)
  • Copy of immunization report (pre-primary)
