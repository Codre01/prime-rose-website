Curriculum

The ultimate goal of the school is to ensure a sound all-round education that will stand the test of time. Prime Rose School operates a combination of Nigerian and British curriculum.  Subjects taught at the nursery level include: Number Work, Letter Work, Phonics, Art & Craft, Sensorial Activities, Rhymes, Story Telling, Science, Moral Instruction, Hand Writing, Understanding the World, Communication and Language Development and Practical life.

Subjects taught at the primary level include: Literacy, Numeracy, History, Pre-Vocational Studies, Basic Science and Technology, Yoruba, Christian Religious Studies, National Values, French, Information Communication Technology (ICT), Vocational Aptitude, Etiquette, Music, Quantitative and Verbal Reasoning, Arts & Crafts and Hand Writing.
