About Us

Prime Rose School

Prime Rose (Montessori) School (“Prime Rose”) was established in September 1986 and draws its rich tradition of over 20 years from its founder, Mrs. A.O. Joseph. The school is located in a quiet close of Gbagada Estate, Phase II in Lagos, Nigeria. At Prime Rose, we aim to recognize every child’s potential at a very early stage and encourage him or her to harness it to the fullest.

Our Experience

The requirements from parents on education have evolved over the years especially as the public education system has collapsed.

During the 1980s, parents were highly satisfied with schools that were content driven and had high moral standards.

In addition, the public secondary systems were still functional and were patronized by many Nigerians.

Key Management
Prime Rose School operates a structure that is highly sensitive to the dynamics of running a school. Due to Prime Rose School’s commitment to providing a conducive learning environment for kids to learn life skills and develop strong characters, the School insists on experienced teachers with very high standards.
The school is presently being administered by a Governing Board made up of 4 individuals who are responsible for setting the broad strategy of the school. The Proprietress is responsible for the day to day running of the school and is being supported by a capable Head Teacher that supervises the core teaching and staff.
Mrs A. O. Joseph
